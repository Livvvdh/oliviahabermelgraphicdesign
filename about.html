about me

My name is Olivia Habermel, I'm 23 years old and I'm a Graphic Designer. After completing my first graduate job in Guildford, I decided to upgrade and move my life to London. This was a huge decision for me as it meant leaving behind my friends and family, my house and the countryside! After finally making the move, Corniavirus hit the country and all of my dreams were put on hold. I couldn't find a job which meant I couldn't afford to rent somewhere leaving me with the option to stay with my Grandma.
